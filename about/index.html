About The Network

Built For Serious Lifters.

Think BIG Bodybuilding Media is a four-show podcast network built from the ground up for competitive bodybuilders, coaches, and serious enhanced lifters. We don't talk about abs and morning smoothies — we talk about contest prep, PEDs, training science, and the reality of life on stage.

Our Mission

Real Answers. No Filter.

The bodybuilding community was drowning in surface-level content — influencers selling programs, unqualified voices spreading misinformation, and mainstream media too afraid to talk about what actually happens behind the scenes of elite-level bodybuilding.

Think BIG exists because serious people deserved a serious network. Every show is hosted by somebody with skin in the game: IFBB Pros who compete at the highest levels, prep coaches with decades of experience, a PhD exercise physiologist, and a harm-reduction specialist. No outsiders. No fluff. No filter.

Over 1,000 episodes later, we're still doing the same thing we started with — answering your questions with real knowledge from real practitioners.

Our Story

From Advices Radio To Think BIG.

The network grew out of Advices Radio — one of the earliest bodybuilding Q&A shows on the internet. What started as one show answering listener questions evolved, over a decade, into a full four-show network covering every angle of competitive bodybuilding.

Blood Sweat & Gear came first — the flagship prep and PED show. Drugs N Stuff expanded the harm-reduction conversation with Dave Crosland's encyclopedic knowledge. It's Just Bodybuilding brought in IFBB Pros Big Ron Partlow and Dusty Hanshaw for the industry and personality angle. Muscle Minds closed the loop with Dr. Scott Stevenson's PhD-level science.

Today, Think BIG is one of the most respected independent networks in the sport — hundreds of thousands of listeners, a Top 1% podcast ranking, and a loyal community of serious lifters who want the real answer, not the safe one.
