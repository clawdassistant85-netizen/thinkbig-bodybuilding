Dr. Scott Stevenson

PhD Exercise Physiology

MM

Dr. Scott Stevenson holds a PhD in exercise physiology and has spent his career bridging the gap between academic research and elite-level bodybuilding practice. He is the author of Fortitude Training, a periodization system widely respected among advanced lifters for its scientific foundation and practical structure. On Muscle Minds, Dr. Stevenson doesn't just cite studies — he explains mechanisms, challenges assumptions, and applies the science to what actually matters inside the gym.

Areas of Focus

  • Muscle physiology and hypertrophy mechanisms
  • Periodization and programming for advanced bodybuilders
  • Peer-reviewed research translation for practical application
  • Fortitude Training methodology and volume management
