Dusty Hanshaw

IFBB Pro

IJB

Dusty Hanshaw earned his IFBB Pro card through years of dedicated competition, developing a reputation for exceptional muscle detail and conditioning. On It's Just Bodybuilding, Dusty provides the counterweight to Big Ron's bluster — thoughtful, historically grounded, and sharp-witted. He's equally comfortable breaking down a competitor's conditioning on stage or debating the greatest bodybuilders of all time.

Areas of Focus

  • IFBB Pro competitive experience and physique development
  • Bodybuilding history and legacy athlete analysis
  • Conditioning assessment and muscle detail critique
  • Training methodology for advanced competitors
